How To With Puff

How To Use Koffii Bottles




To use Koffii bottle you need to follow these steps:
1- press the cover to open the bottle to drink
2- press the cover again to close to bottle

Important note & tips

*For hot drinks you should first add boiled water, as Koffii is coated with ceramic . This is how it will maintain the cold

*For cold drinks you need to add ice first, as Koffii is coated with ceramic . This is how it will maintain the cold








To clean Puff bottle, you need to follow these steps:
1- Take your Puff apart
2- Wash each piece with warm water, dish soap, and a soft sponge
3- Soak it in hot/warm water and add baking soda or a stainless steel cleaning tablet
4- Let it air dry
5- Put it back together and enjoy!

Important note & tips

*Clean your bottle regularly to extend its life and get the maximum beneift from it

*For a quick clean soak the bottle for 8 minutes

*For deep clean soak the bottle overnight
